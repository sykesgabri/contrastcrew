Hello, welcome to the Contrast Crew website.
We are a nerf community focused on helping people, whether they be newcomers or experienced nerfers. We exist as a convenient hub of nerf-related information for you to use and rely upon. We also host nerf events and give advice on hosting events as well.


We encourage people to join the community, and we ancourage people to enjoy themselves here. Nerf isn't just for little kids, it is for everyone.
So please explore the site, learn as much as you wish, get your friends involved, and most importantly, have fun.
